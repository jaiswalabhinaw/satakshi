About the Founder

"Service is the greatest religion."

Early Life & Turning Point

Mr. Naveen Ranjan Srivastava was born in Manikpur, Gopalganj. His early dreams were academic (IAS), but the tragic loss of his younger brother and the pain he witnessed redirected his path to social service. In 2011 he founded Shatakshi Seva Sansthan to serve humanity.

Key Milestones

  • 2011 — Foundation of Shatakshi.
  • 2012 — Antim Vidai: dignified funerals for abandoned bodies (900+ so far).
  • 2019 — From Weapons to Pens: education for flood-affected children (800+).
  • 2020 — Voice of Silence & Jal Pathshala: women & environment programs.
  • 2020 — Shatakshi Helpline: 24×7 assistance to access government schemes.
  • 2021 — COVID relief: ambulance services, rations, medicines.
  • 2024 — Annapurna Rasoi: daily meals for elders across 15 villages.

Personality & Support

Naveenji is a symbol of compassion and sacrifice; supported by his wife Mrs. Shipli Saxena and the trust of the community, he continues serving tirelessly.
