FAQ

Common questions on volunteering, donations and partnerships.

How can I volunteer?
Sign up via the Volunteer link or contact us directly — we will guide you through training and schedules.
Are donations tax-exempt?
Yes — donations are covered under 80G/12A where applicable. We provide receipts for donors.
Can corporates partner via CSR?
Yes. We welcome CSR partnerships — please contact our partnerships team for proposals and impact reporting.
