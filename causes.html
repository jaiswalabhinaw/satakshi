Our Objectives & Causes

Shatakshi works across multiple dimensions to bring change to every corner of society. Below are our key initiatives.

🎓 Education for All — quality education for every child.
👩‍⚕️ Rural Health — check-ups & medicine distribution.
👵 Senior Citizen Services — care for helpless elders.
💧 Water Conservation — campaigns to save water.
🌱 Tree Plantation — mass planting drives.
🍲 Annapurna Rasoi — free meals for vulnerable people.
